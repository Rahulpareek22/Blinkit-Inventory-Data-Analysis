# Blinkit-Inventory-Data-Analysis

##  Overview

This project analyzes inventory and sales data from Blinkit to uncover actionable insights for improving inventory efficiency, reducing stock imbalances, and optimizing product availability.

In quick-commerce platforms, efficient inventory management is critical to ensure high product availability while minimizing wastage and storage costs. This project applies data analysis techniques to solve real-world inventory challenges.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze sales and demand patterns across products and categories
* Identify overstocked and understocked items
* Measure inventory efficiency using key performance metrics
* Detect stockout risks and slow-moving products
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

##  Dataset Description

The dataset contains:

* Product details (category, pricing, etc.)
* Inventory levels
* Sales and demand data
* Expiry-related information

---

##  Key Features & Metrics

*  **Inventory Turnover Ratio** – Measures how efficiently stock is sold
*  **Demand-Stock Gap** – Difference between demand and available inventory
*  **Stockout Risk Indicator** – Identifies products likely to go out of stock
*  **Overstock Indicator** – Detects excess inventory
*  **Days to Expiry / Product Age** – Tracks perishable product lifecycle

---

##  Key Insights

* Several products show **low inventory turnover**, indicating inefficiency
* Certain categories face frequent **stockout risks**
* Overstocking exists in slow-moving items, increasing costs
* Demand patterns vary significantly across categories

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Prioritize restocking for fast-moving products
* Optimize inventory levels using demand patterns
* Use discounts/promotions to clear overstocked items
* Improve forecasting for better demand planning
